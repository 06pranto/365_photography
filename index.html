About this Library

The journey of this library started at the end of 2020 intending to make the young generation interested in photography books. The main objective was not only the book but also the history of photography in this country and creating interest in the work of legendary photographers of Bangladesh. The hope was that children would come and learn by reading or watching books. The library now has about 1,100 books on photography, plus more than 500 photography magazines and exhibition catalogs. Besides, there are other things including posters, and calendars. With everyone's support, there is a plan to take this library to a more prominent place.

Book Catagory

Bangladesh

All kinds of photo book and magazines from almost every photographer from Bangladesh published by local and international publishers. Including the very first book of respected photographer M.A Beg sir and all the books from various art and photography institution of Bangladesh. Come and enjoy the light of photography of Bangladeshi photographer.

Foreigner

We’ve been digging through some of the most beautiful international photobooks out there! Check out these breathtakingly beautiful photobooks from our friends around the world. We invite you to explore the world through the master's eyes.

Art & Cinema

For the past few years, companion film art and making-of books have risen in popularity. There’s a lot of design to draw from — and with that, a lot of design work to fill a book. However, over the last decade, we’ve also seen art books accompany films outside of the animated and superhero genres. cCome and explore what you like the most.

NGO's

A book can be a powerful tool in the fight for human rights. We believe in the power of storytelling to inspire and change the world. A huge number of NGO is working towards a future where every child has access to quality education, healthcare and protection. See and learn how a photo captures a moment in time, and tells a story.

magazines

A magazine can be a photographer's best friend. Whether you're looking for inspiration, tips, or just wanting to keep up with the latest trends, a photography magazine is the perfect way to get your fix. We've got all the latest and greatest photography magazines right here. So whether you're into fashion, film, or wildlife photography, we've got something for you!
